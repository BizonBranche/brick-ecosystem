‚ùì Frequently Asked Question

"Is my message on the blockchain right now?"

Great question! Here's how it works:

  1. Now: Your image and message are securely stored in our database, linked to your specific cell (B7, F12, etc.)
  2. When the canvas fills up: Once all 400 cells are sold, we mint the ENTIRE canvas as an NFT collection on Solana
  3. Forever after: Each cell becomes a genuine, tradable NFT on the Solana blockchain. YOUR message becomes immortal.
Why not directly on blockchain? Storing images directly is expensive (could cost 10x more per cell). This way, you pay 0.1 SOL instead of 1+ SOL, and STILL get immortality when the canvas completes.

So yes - your memory WILL be on the blockchain forever. Just with a clever, cost-efficient path to get there.
